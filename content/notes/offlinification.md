It's been some years since I've decided to have whatever i use online, locally on my machine. in a normal country you'd ask why? and most of the time you're right. there are not that many convincing reasons to localize everything.

but, -and this is a really big but- in countries like Iran that we had 3 internet blackouts in a single year, you'd better think twice.

I call it "offlinification" and Kiwix is the one software I use the most. you can have Wikipedia and/or any other documentation -offline- with Kiwix.
